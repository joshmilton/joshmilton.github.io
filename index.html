Introduction

For this assessment task I have explored and critically evaluated the theories and frameworks of the Cognitive Theory of Multimedia Learning (Mayer, 2014) and Cognitive Load Theory (Sweller, 1998) as applicable to the realm of Music Education.

Using the principles of these two theories I have applied these to the design of a multimedia platform for creation of Music Education multimedia. This has been done as a means of improving upon Music education practices in the digital space through clearer instructional design and improved accessibility and interactivity.


Context and Definitions

Formal Music Learning

Formal learners in this context refers to learners who are undertaking study in either an educational institution (secondary or tertiary) or through an online provider in a course format (MOOC, etc.).
For these learners it is assumed that they are interacting with music education multimedia in a structured fashion such as an online course or classroom environment.
