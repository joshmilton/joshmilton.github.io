Introduction

In this assessment task I have explored the theories and frameworks of Cognitive Theory of Multimedia Learning (Mayer, 2014) and Cognitive Load Theory (Sweller, 1998) and applied these to the design of a multimedia platform for creation of Music Education multimedia. This has been done as a means of improving upon Music education practices in the digital space through clearer instructional design and improved accessibility and interactivity.
